Matilda

At 19, Matilda lives on a cattle farm in Australia. She's passionate about the tough work she does and values land care, social equity, and fair trade. Concerned about issues of isolation, mental health, and climate change, she feels distant from urban living despite her strong ties to her local community. Pursuing online studies in Environmental Science, funded by her family and farm work, she prefers simple and fast website designs due to her slower internet speed, cherishing the beauty and challenges that come with country living.

Sofia

An artist residing in the Swiss Alps, Sofia wishes for a platform to display her woodworking and traditional sculptures, inspired by her family's legacy. Deeply connected with her surroundings, she values environmental conservation and being conservative, advocating for sustainable living. Juggling her artistic endeavors while completing an arts degree, she favors fast and intuitive websites, seeking serenity and inspiration amidst the tranquil mountain views.
